FAQ

Logistics, comfort, and calm.

How delivery works

We tow the sauna to your location on a trailer, level it on a flat surface (driveway, yard, or event space), and give you a full walkthrough of the wood stove, temperature controls, and best practices. Setup typically takes 30–45 minutes. When your rental ends, we handle pickup and breakdown—no cleanup required on your end.

Capacity

The barrel comfortably seats 6–8 people at once, but can support up to 12 people cycling in and out. Most people do 10–20 minute rounds followed by cool-down breaks. This keeps the experience comfortable and lets everyone enjoy the heat without overcrowding.

Suggested temperatures

Wood-fired saunas can reach temps gym saunas can't. For casual conversation and 20-minute sessions, aim for 158°F (70°C). Standard sauna sessions work best at 176°F (80°C) for 15 minutes. For intense 10-minute rounds, push to 194°F+ (90°C+). Expert-level heat at 212°F+ (100°C+) is 100% possible with wood-fired saunas—something electric units simply can't achieve.

Heat-up time

Heat-up time depends on outside temperature, your target sauna temp, and how attentive you are to the fire. The wood stove heats at roughly 72°F per hour (40°C/hour). For example, starting at 20°F and reaching 180°F takes about 2 hours if you know what you're doing and stay on top of it, or closer to 3 hours if you're a bit more relaxed about maintaining the fire. We'll show you how to build and maintain the fire, and you can start heating it up as soon as we finish setup.

What to bring

Essentials: towels, water bottles, and comfortable clothing or swimwear. Many people bring robes for between rounds, flip-flops for outside, and cold drinks. If you want the full experience, consider a cold plunge tub (we can discuss options) or just use a nearby lake, pool, or even a cold shower.

Safety

Stay hydrated, take breaks when you need them, and listen to your body. Avoid alcohol before or during sauna sessions. If you're pregnant, have heart conditions, or other health concerns, consult your doctor first. We provide safety guidelines and are available by text if you have questions during your rental.

Cancellations

Life happens. We offer flexible rescheduling with at least 48 hours notice. For weather-related concerns (extreme cold, storms), we'll work with you to reschedule or adjust. The security deposit is fully refundable as long as the sauna is returned in the same condition.

Cleaning

We handle all cleaning. After pickup, we do a full sanitization, wipe down all surfaces, and refresh the cedar interior. Each rental starts with a clean, fresh sauna. You just need to make sure the fire is fully extinguished and the sauna is accessible for pickup.

Weather considerations

The sauna works year-round. In winter, we ensure clear access paths and can discuss snow removal if needed. Summer rentals benefit from shade setups and ventilation. The wood stove keeps it warm even in freezing temps, and the barrel design holds heat efficiently. We'll coordinate with you based on forecast conditions.
